Barbell Bench Press

4 × 6-8 reps
Rest: 2.5 min
Muscles🟢 Chest  ·  🔵 Anterior Delt  ·  🔵 Tricep

Retract shoulder blades hard into the bench before every rep. Lower in 3 seconds to mid-chest — this slow eccentric is where muscle fiber damage happens, which drives growth. Drive explosively up. Squeeze chest at the top. This is your primary mass builder.

Equipment not available? See alternatives
Dumbbell Bench PressSame movement, greater ROM. Use ~60-70% of your barbell weight. Same sets/reps.
Machine Chest PressGreat when the rack is occupied. Reduce load ~15%. Same sets/reps.
Weighted Push-UpPlate on upper back or band across back. 3×12-15 slow reps.

Incline Dumbbell Press

3 × 8-10 reps
Rest: 2 min
Muscles🟢 Upper Chest  ·  🔵 Anterior Delt

Set bench to 30-45°. Lower wide in 3 seconds — feel the deep stretch across your upper chest. Press to full extension. Upper chest (clavicular head) creates that full, thick look from collarbone downward.

Equipment not available? See alternatives
Incline Barbell PressBarbell version. Same stimulus. Same sets/reps.
Low-to-High Cable FlyAnchor at hip height, pull up and together. 3×12-15.
Smith Machine Incline PressGood when dumbbells are taken. Same sets/reps.

Cable Lateral Raise

3 × 12-15 reps
Rest: 90 sec
Muscles🟢 Medial Delt  ·  🔵 Anterior Delt

Cable provides constant tension unlike dumbbells. Lead with your elbow, not wrist. Raise to just above shoulder height — don’t shrug. Control the return in 3 seconds. Wide, capped shoulders are built here. Do both arms.

Equipment not available? See alternatives
Dumbbell Lateral RaiseLead with elbow, slight forward lean. Same sets/reps.
Machine Lateral RaiseEliminates cheat. Focus on squeeze at the top.
Band Lateral RaiseStep on band with opposite foot. 3×20.

Rope Tricep Pushdown

3 × 12-15 reps
Rest: 90 sec
Muscles🟢 Tricep (lateral head)  ·  🔵 Tricep (medial head)

Pin elbows to your sides throughout. At the bottom, flare the rope ends outward for peak contraction. Slow 3-second return. Triceps = 2/3 of upper arm mass. This is how you get big arms.

Equipment not available? See alternatives
V-Bar or Straight Bar PushdownSame movement, different attachment. Same sets/reps.
Overhead DB Tricep ExtensionTargets long head. Lower behind head in 3 seconds. 3×10-12.
Bench Dips (weighted)Plate on lap. 3×12-15.

Face Pull

2 × 15-20 reps
Rest: 60 sec
Muscles🟢 Rear Delt  ·  🟢 External Rotator (rotator cuff)

Set cable at face height. Pull toward your face with elbows HIGH and flared. This counterbalances all the pressing volume. Shoulder health exercise — keep it light. Never skip it or shoulder impingement will follow over time.

Equipment not available? See alternatives
Band Face PullAnchor band at face height. Same movement. 2×20-25.
DB Reverse Fly (bent over)Hinge at hips, raise dumbbells to sides. 3×12-15.
Cable Rear Delt FlyCables crossed at face height, pull outward. 3×12-15.
