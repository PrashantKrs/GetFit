Hack Squat

3 × 10-12 reps
Rest: 2.5 min
Muscles🟢 Rectus Femoris + Vastus Lateralis (outer sweep) · 🔵 Glutes

Feet low and close on the platform — more upright torso = more rectus femoris. Full ROM: descend until hips are below knees. The hack squat allows a more vertical shin than a back squat, maximally loading the rectus femoris and vastus lateralis (outer quad sweep). 5 different quad angles across the week builds complete quad development.

Seated Leg Curl

3 × 12-15 reps
Rest: 90 sec
Muscles🟢 Hamstrings — inner/short head (knee-dominant) · 🔵 Gastrocnemius

The seated position flexes the hip, which pre-stretches the hamstrings — research shows this produces more hypertrophy than lying leg curl. Full ROM: complete extension, then curl fully. Tue = RDL (hip-dominant hamstring), Fri = seated leg curl (knee-dominant). Two different hamstring loading patterns = complete development.

Equipment not available? See alternatives

Nordic Hamstring CurlKneel, anchor feet, lower body slowly. 3×6-8.
Swiss Ball Leg CurlLie on back, feet on ball, curl toward hips. 3×12-15.

Arnold Press

3 × 10-12 reps
Rest: 90 sec
Muscles🟢 All 3 Delt Heads (anterior, medial, posterior) · 🔵 Tricep

Start with palms facing you (like top of a curl), rotate outward as you press to finish palms-forward overhead. The rotation recruits all three delt heads through the arc — the only single shoulder exercise that does so. Mon = medial delt (cable lateral), Wed = ant+med (OHP), Fri = all 3 heads (Arnold). Full delt coverage.

Equipment not available? See alternatives

Machine Shoulder PressGood for removing stabilizer fatigue. Same sets/reps.
Landmine PressArc-based press, great shoulder-friendly option. 3×10-12 each side.

Chest-Supported DB Row

3 × 10-12 reps
Rest: 2 min
Muscles🟢 Rhomboids + Mid-Back · 🔵 Lats · 🔵 Rear Delt

Incline bench at 45°, chest against pad. Hang DBs, pull to hips with elbows flared wide — this targets the rhomboids and mid-trap specifically. Chest support eliminates lower back fatigue after a heavy week, allowing pure back contraction focus. Squeeze hard for 1 second at the top. Friday's row completes the week's back coverage.

Equipment not available? See alternatives

Seated Cable Row (wide grip)Wide grip bar, elbows out. Same sets/reps.
Meadows RowLandmine attachment, single arm, elbow flared. 3×10-12 each.

Low-to-High Cable Fly

3 × 12-15 reps
Rest: 90 sec
Muscles🟢 Upper Inner Chest (clavicular head, adduction) · 🔵 Anterior Delt

Cable anchored at hip height. Pull upward and across your body — hands meet at chin height. The upward arc maximally recruits the clavicular (upper) pec head through its adduction function. Flies target horizontal adduction which pressing exercises can't fully replicate. Keep a slight elbow bend throughout, no locking.

Equipment not available? See alternatives

Pec Deck (upper)Adjust arms high. Same sets/reps.
Push-Up PlusAt top of push-up, add a protraction. 3×15.

Concentration Curl

3 × 12-15 reps
Rest: 90 sec
Muscles🟢 Bicep Short Head (peak squeeze) · 🔵 Brachialis

Sit, elbow braced on inner thigh — eliminates all momentum. Curl with full supination at the top (pinky rotates outward). The braced position and shoulder-flexed angle target the short head for peak contraction. EMG research shows concentration curl produces the highest bicep activation of any curl variation. End the week with a focused pump.

Equipment not available? See alternatives

High-Cable CurlCable at face height, curl toward forehead. 3×12-15.
Machine Preacher CurlArm pad version. Same sets/reps.

Hanging Leg Raise + Side Plank

3 rounds
Rest: 60 sec
Muscles🟢 Rectus Abdominis · 🟢 Obliques (side plank) · 🔵 Hip Flexors

Hanging Leg Raise: hang from bar, raise straight legs to 90° (or bent knees if needed). 10-15 reps. Immediately → Side Plank: forearm on floor, body rigid, hold 30 seconds each side. The hanging raise trains rectus abdominis through a large ROM; the side plank targets obliques for rotational stability. Together: complete core every Friday.
