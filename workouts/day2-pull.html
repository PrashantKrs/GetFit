Pull-up / Lat Pulldown

4 × 6-8 reps
Rest: 2.5 min
Muscles🟢 Lats  ·  🟢 Upper Back  ·  🔵 Bicep

Use pull-ups if you can do 6+ clean reps (add weight via belt if you can do 12+). For pulldowns: think “pull elbows down to your hips” not “pull bar down.” This activates lats instead of arms. 3-second eccentric on the way up.

Equipment not available? See alternatives

Barbell Bent-Over Row

3 × 8-10 reps
Rest: 2 min
Muscles🟢 Mid-Back  ·  🟢 Lats  ·  🔵 Rear Delt  ·  🔵 Bicep

Hinge to ~45°, brace your core hard (this protects your spine). Overhand grip. Pull bar to your lower chest — not your belly. Full scapular retraction at the top. 3-second eccentric. This is the back’s bench press — it builds thickness.

Equipment not available? See alternatives
Single-Arm Dumbbell RowKnee on bench. Full ROM stretch at bottom. Same sets/reps each arm.
T-Bar RowSimilar movement. Great for mid-back thickness. Same sets/reps.
Chest-Supported DB RowIncline bench removes lower back fatigue. Same sets/reps.

Seated Cable Row

3 × 10-12 reps
Rest: 2 min
Muscles🟢 Mid-Back  ·  🔵 Bicep  ·  🔵 Forearm

Sit tall, chest up. Pull to your lower chest — squeeze your shoulder blades together hard at the peak. Let your scapulas fully protract on the way back out for full ROM. 3-second eccentric. Builds the mid-back thickness that makes your back look 3D.

Equipment not available? See alternatives
Machine RowChest-supported. Same stimulus. Same sets/reps.
Band RowAnchor band to a post. Same pull pattern. 3×15-20.
Single-Arm DB RowKnee on bench, full ROM. 3×10-12 each arm.

Hammer Curl

3 × 12-15 reps
Rest: 90 sec
Muscles🟢 Brachioradialis  ·  🟢 Forearm  ·  🔵 Bicep

Neutral grip (thumbs up). This targets the brachioradialis and the outer forearm more than a standard curl. Keep elbows pinned. 3-second lowering. Forearm thickness shows from every angle — this is the primary builder.

Equipment not available? See alternatives
Cross-Body Hammer CurlSame neutral grip, curl across the body. Slightly different angle. Same sets/reps.
Rope Hammer Curl (cable)Constant tension version. 3×12-15.
EZ-Bar Curl (neutral grip)Reduces wrist stress. Same stimulus. Same sets/reps.

Reverse Wrist Curl

2 × 15-20 reps
Rest: 60 sec
Muscles🟢 Forearm Extensors (wrist extensors)

Overhand grip, forearms resting on thighs. Extend wrists upward, lower slowly. Use light weight — these muscles are small. Forearm flexors are trained on every pull exercise; extensors are neglected, causing imbalance and tennis elbow over time. This fixes that.

Equipment not available? See alternatives
Reverse DB CurlOverhand grip on dumbbell curl. Wrist extensors + brachioradialis. 2×15-20.
Band Wrist ExtensionAnchor band under foot, extend wrist against resistance. 2×20.
Reverse Cable CurlCable machine, overhand grip. Constant tension. 2×15-20.
