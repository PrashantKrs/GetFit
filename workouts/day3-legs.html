Exercises — legs respond best to full ROM and controlled eccentrics

Barbell Back Squat

4 × 6-8 reps
Rest: 3 min
Muscles🟢 Quads  ·  🟢 Glutes  ·  🔵 Hamstrings  ·  🔵 Core

Descend below parallel (hip crease below knee). Chest stays up, brace your core like you’re about to get punched. Drive through your heels on the way up. 3-second eccentric down. The king of mass builders — no substitute drives leg growth like the squat.

Equipment not available? See alternatives
Goblet SquatDumbbell or kettlebell held at chest. Great for form. 4×10-12.
Hack Squat MachineQuad-dominant, similar ROM. Same sets/reps.
Safety Bar SquatIf regular bar is uncomfortable. Same movement. Same sets/reps.

Leg Press

3 × 10-12 reps
Rest: 2 min
Muscles🟢 Quads  ·  🔵 Glutes

Feet shoulder-width, mid-plate. Lower until your knees reach ~90° — don’t let your lower back peel off the pad. Press to just short of lockout (keeps tension on quads). 3-second eccentric. Adds volume after the squat without further spine loading.

Equipment not available? See alternatives
Hack Squat MachineMore quad-dominant than leg press. Same sets/reps.
Bulgarian Split SquatRear foot elevated, dumbbell in each hand. 3×10-12 each leg.
Goblet Squat (high rep)If no machines. 3×15-20 with controlled tempo.

Romanian Deadlift

3 × 10-12 reps
Rest: 2 min
Muscles🟢 Hamstrings  ·  🟢 Glutes  ·  🔵 Spinal Erectors

Hip hinge — push your hips BACK, not down. Bar stays close to your legs the whole way. Lower until you feel a deep hamstring stretch (usually mid-shin). 3-second eccentric. The hamstring stretch under load is the primary hypertrophy stimulus here.

Equipment not available? See alternatives
Dumbbell RDLSame movement with dumbbells. Allows a deeper stretch. Same sets/reps.
Single-Leg RDLUnilateral. Dumbbells in each hand. Better stretch + balance work. 3×10-12 each.
Cable Pull-ThroughHip hinge with cable. Constant tension on glutes and hamstrings. 3×12-15.

Leg Curl (Lying or Seated)

3 × 12-15 reps
Rest: 90 sec
Muscles🟢 Hamstrings (isolation)

Curl heels toward glutes, hold 1 second squeeze at the peak. 3-second eccentric on the way down. Seated version provides better stretch at the lengthened position (hip is flexed). Either version works — use whichever machine is available.

Equipment not available? See alternatives
Seated Leg CurlDifferent machine, same muscles. Seated version has better lengthened position.
Nordic Hamstring CurlKneel on a pad, anchor feet, lower slowly under control. 3×6-8 (very hard).
Stability Ball Leg CurlLie on floor, heels on ball, curl ball toward glutes. 3×12-15.

Calf Superset

3 rounds · 15-20 reps each
Rest: 90 sec between rounds
Muscles🟢 Gastrocnemius (standing)  ·  🟢 Soleus (seated)

Do 15-20 standing calf raises IMMEDIATELY into 15-20 seated calf raises. Rest 90 sec. Repeat 3 rounds. Standing = gastrocnemius (the visible outer calf). Seated = soleus (pushes gastrocnemius outward). Training only one leaves the calf underdeveloped. Full ROM — all the way up, all the way down for a deep stretch.

Equipment not available? See alternatives
Smith Machine Calf RaiseFor standing raises if the calf raise machine is taken.
Leg Press Calf RaiseToes on edge of leg press platform. Excellent stretch. Both gastroc and soleus.
Step Calf Raise (bodyweight)Hang heel off a step, single or double leg. 3×20-25 with 3-second pause at bottom.
