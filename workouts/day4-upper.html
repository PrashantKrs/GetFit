Seated DB Overhead Press

4 × 8-10 reps
Rest: 2.5 min
Muscles🟢 All Delt Heads  ·  🔵 Tricep  ·  🔵 Trap (upper)

Start with dumbbells at shoulder height. Press in a slight arc to full overhead lockout. 3-second lowering. Seated removes leg drive (more shoulder isolation than standing). Dumbbells allow a natural arc vs barbell’s fixed path — better for shoulder health.

Equipment not available? See alternatives
Barbell Overhead PressSlightly less natural arc. Same stimulus. Same sets/reps.
Smith Machine OHPFixed path. Use when dumbbells are occupied. Same sets/reps.
Machine Shoulder PressGuided path. Good if shoulder discomfort with free weights. Same sets/reps.

Arnold Press

3 × 10-12 reps
Rest: 2 min
Muscles🟢 All Delt Heads (rotational coverage)

Start with dumbbells at chin height, palms facing YOU. As you press up, rotate palms to face FORWARD by the time arms are extended. 3-second lowering, rotate back. The rotation engages all three delt heads through the ROM — better shoulder roundness vs standard press.

Equipment not available? See alternatives
Seated DB OHP (standard)Skip rotation. Just add another set of standard OHP instead. 4 total sets.
Landmine PressAngled press. Great for shoulder health. 3×10-12 each arm.

Incline Dumbbell Curl

3 × 10-12 reps
Rest: 90 sec
Muscles🟢 Bicep (long head)  ·  🔵 Brachialis

Set bench to 45-60°. Arms hang naturally behind your body — this pre-stretches the bicep long head. Curl to full supination (pinky rotates up at the top). 3-second lowering. The long head is the outer bicep that creates the peak — this is the best stretch-position curl for it.

Equipment not available? See alternatives
Cable Curl (low pulley)Constant tension. 3×12-15.
Preacher CurlIsolates at the shortened position. Good paired with incline for full ROM coverage. Same sets/reps.
Concentration CurlSeated, elbow braced on thigh. High peak contraction. 3×10-12.

Reverse Barbell Curl

3 × 12-15 reps
Rest: 90 sec
Muscles🟢 Brachioradialis  ·  🟢 Forearm Extensors  ·  🔵 Bicep

Overhand (pronated) grip. Use a lighter weight than standard curls — the brachioradialis is a smaller muscle. Keep elbows pinned. 3-second lowering. This is the second forearm extensor stimulus this week (Day 2 had wrist curls). Builds the outer forearm thickness.

Equipment not available? See alternatives
Reverse DB CurlSame movement with dumbbells. Allows independent arm action. Same sets/reps.
Reverse Cable CurlConstant tension. 3×12-15.
EZ-Bar Reverse CurlReduces wrist strain vs straight bar. Same sets/reps.

EZ-Bar Overhead Tricep Extension

3 × 10-12 reps
Rest: 90 sec
Muscles🟢 Tricep (long head)  ·  🔵 Tricep (medial head)

Arms straight overhead, lower EZ-bar behind your head in 3 seconds — feel the deep long head stretch. Keep elbows close to your head (don’t flare). The long head is the largest head of the tricep and is ONLY fully stretched in the overhead position — this makes it the best mass builder for the tricep.

Equipment not available? See alternatives
DB Overhead Tricep ExtensionOne dumbbell, both hands. Same movement. Same sets/reps.
Cable Overhead Extension (rope)Cable from low pulley, face away, extend overhead. Constant tension. 3×12-15.
Skull Crusher (lying)Lower bar to forehead. Hits long head differently. 3×10-12.
