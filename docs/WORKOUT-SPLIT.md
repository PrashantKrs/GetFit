# 5-Day Hypertrophy Workout Split

**Schedule:** Monday – Friday | **Session:** 45 min (9:30–10:15 am) | **Goal:** Aesthetic muscle gain

## Day 1 — Push (Monday)
*Primary: Chest, Anterior Delt, Medial Delt, Triceps*

| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Rest | Primary | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Barbell Bench Press | 4 × 6-8 | 2.5 min | Chest | Compound anchor; 3s eccentric |
| Incline DB Press | 3 × 8-10 | 2 min | Upper Chest | 30-45° incline; builds thickness |
| Cable Lateral Raise | 3 × 12-15 | 90 sec | Medial Delt | Constant tension; lead with elbow |
| Rope Tricep Pushdown | 3 × 12-15 | 90 sec | Tricep | Flare rope at bottom for peak contraction |
| Face Pull | 2 × 15-20 | 60 sec | Rear Delt / RC | Shoulder health; never skip |

**Session estimate:** ~44 min

---

## Day 2 — Pull (Tuesday)
*Primary: Back (lats, mid-back), Biceps, Forearms*

| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Rest | Primary | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Pull-up / Lat Pulldown | 4 × 6-8 | 2.5 min | Lats, Upper Back | Add weight if BW pull-ups > 12 reps |
| Barbell Bent-Over Row | 3 × 8-10 | 2 min | Mid-back, Lats | Overhand; brace core hard |
| Seated Cable Row | 3 × 10-12 | 2 min | Mid-back, Bicep | Full scapular retraction |
| Hammer Curl | 3 × 12-15 | 90 sec | Brachioradialis, Forearm | Neutral grip; builds forearm thickness |
| Reverse Wrist Curl | 2 × 15-20 | 60 sec | Forearm Extensors | Often neglected; critical for elbow health |

**Session estimate:** ~45 min

---

## Day 3 — Legs (Wednesday)
*Primary: Quads, Hamstrings, Calves*

| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Rest | Primary | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Barbell Back Squat | 4 × 6-8 | 3 min | Quads, Glutes | Below parallel; beltless until form breaks |
| Leg Press | 3 × 10-12 | 2 min | Quads, Glutes | Feet shoulder-width mid-plate |
| Romanian Deadlift | 3 × 10-12 | 2 min | Hamstrings, Glutes | Hip hinge; bar stays close to legs |
| Leg Curl (lying/seated) | 3 × 12-15 | 90 sec | Hamstrings | Controlled eccentric |
| Calf Superset* | 3 rounds | 90 sec | Gastrocnemius + Soleus | *See note below |

*Calf Superset: 15-20 reps Standing Calf Raise (gastrocnemius) immediately into 15-20 reps Seated Calf Raise (soleus). Rest 90s then repeat. Both heads must be trained — gastrocnemius crosses the knee, soleus does not.

**Session estimate:** ~45 min

---

## Day 4 — Upper (Thursday)
*Primary: Shoulders (all heads), Biceps, Forearms, Triceps — second frequency hit for arms*

| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Rest | Primary | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Seated DB Overhead Press | 4 × 8-10 | 2.5 min | All Delt Heads, Tricep | Natural arc; full overhead lock-out |
| Arnold Press | 3 × 10-12 | 2 min | All Delt Heads | Rotational; great for medial + anterior coverage |
| Incline DB Curl | 3 × 10-12 | 90 sec | Bicep (long head) | Supinate fully at top; long head = peak |
| Reverse Barbell Curl | 3 × 12-15 | 90 sec | Brachioradialis, Forearm | Forearm extensors; overhand grip |
| EZ-Bar Overhead Tricep Extension | 3 × 10-12 | 90 sec | Tricep (long head) | Full stretch at bottom; long head = mass |

**Session estimate:** ~44 min

---

## Day 5 — Lower B (Friday)
*Primary: Hamstrings, Glutes, Calves, Core | Deadlift as anchor compound*

| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Rest | Primary | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Conventional Deadlift | 4 × 4-6 | 3-4 min | Full Posterior Chain, Grip | Heaviest lift of the week; brace hard |
| Hip Thrust (barbell) | 3 × 10-12 | 2 min | Glutes, Hip Extensors | Chin tucked, drive through heels |
| Lying Leg Curl | 3 × 12-15 | 90 sec | Hamstrings | Second hamstring hit this week |
| Seated Calf Raise | 4 × 15-20 | 90 sec | Soleus | Soleus = deeper calf muscle; seated isolates it |
| Core Circuit | 2 rounds | 60 sec | Abs, Obliques | Plank 45s + Hanging Leg Raise 10-12 reps |

**Session estimate:** ~43 min
